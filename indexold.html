Welcome to [reboot]!

Creativity Through Collaboration

[reboot] is a student-run club under the Federation of Students at UW that was founded in late 2014 by a group of first year students. The club is working to prove that the most unique, and most creative ideas come from a collaboration between students from all faculties, not simply from one group of students. Instead of each faculty working individually on homogenous goals, our members work together to showcase the best of each faculty. We are redefining the culture of cretion.

2015 Collaborative Project


Members of the club came together at our kickoff meeting in February to create the concept for our 2015 term project. The goal was to build a symbol that represents the creativity, ambition, and ‘wow-factor’ that can come out of a collaborative effort from students from all faculties.
We will be creating an interactive, moving, and visually evolving sculpture. Students across campus will be able to change how the sculpture moves and looks by creating music from an app that we will design; the music being created from anywhere on campus will be heard at the sculpture, and the sculpture itself will react to the tones, melodies, and rhythms of the music by changing shape and colour.
The project will allow each member of the club to showcase their individual skills, as well as to learn from others. The project itself is something which has never before been seen at the University of Waterloo, and will allow any student on campus to add their personality to our central sculpture.
