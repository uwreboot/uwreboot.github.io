The Club

Creativity through collaboration; we’re a community of people working to prove that the best ideas come from sharing experiences. Our members grow together, learning from each other while taking part in hands-on projects that push them out of their comfort zone. Nobody is an expert in everything, but everyone is skilled at something. This is an opportunity to discover something new firsthand-- by doing it. This is where you learn something, you teach something, and together you create something amazing. It’s time to show that innovation and creativity go hand-in-hand. It’s time to unite those who want their ideas to become a reality. It’s time to reboot the culture of creation.

What we do

We’ll give you some materials, tools, and a space to use them in. You’ve got your experience and your know-how, but you’ve also got an awesome group of people around you to learn from. Together, by sharing your skills, you have two hours to design, create, and build something that you're proud of; hands-on learning at its finest. Let’s see what this club can come up with.

Have an idea for a cool project? Make it come to life by letting us know.
